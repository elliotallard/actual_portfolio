I study information systems and human computer interaction at Carnegie Mellon.

Hi, I'm Elliot. I love people, user-centered design, and computers.

I am fascinated by what the technology of today is capable of, whether it be social media platforms, warehouse robots equipped with machine learning, or even facial recognition software. At the same time, I am incredibly invested in the people affected by these developments, whether it be economically, culturally, or psychologically.

With that said, I want to be part of technology/experience generation with a focus on the humans who would be using or affected by it. Our world needs it.
